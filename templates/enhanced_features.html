Best Practices for Optimal Results
✓ Recommended Conditions
  • Well-lit environment (natural or bright artificial light)
  • Clear, unobstructed view of faces
  • Faces at least 50x50 pixels in size
  • Multiple training images per student for better accuracy
  • Consistent camera angles and distances
✗ Conditions to Avoid
  • Extreme lighting conditions (too dark/bright)
  • Heavy shadows across faces
  • Blurry or low-resolution images
  • Faces partially covered (masks, hats, hands)
  • Extreme camera angles or distances
